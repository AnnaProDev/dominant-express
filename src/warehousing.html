what is WAREHOUSING

Warehousing involves storing goods or materials before distribution, encompassing receiving, storing, and distributing business goods. Dominant Express offers reliable storage and logistics services, managing the flow of goods from start to finish, reducing inefficiencies and ensuring trustworthy supply-chain management. Here are a few existing types of warehouses:

public warehouses

Public warehouses offer short-term storage space for businesses. They have flexible pricing to suit changing needs. Small businesses and start-ups often use shared warehouse space to save money. Larger companies might use public warehouses for extra storage or to make room during changes or upgrades.

private warehouses

Private warehouses, owned by big companies, need a lot of money upfront but save money in the long run. They're placed strategically near factories or busy places to prepare stock for busy times or keep products available.

contract warehouse

A contract warehouse offers businesses space rental for storing goods over several years, providing stability for consistent needs. Alternatively, shared warehouses suit those with fluctuating requirements. Businesses can request exclusivity or operational control.

distribution center

Distribution centers quickly distribute large product volumes to retailers, meeting short-term storage needs. Depending on the product, shipments may spend less than a day here. These centers play a crucial role in the supply chain by swiftly moving goods from large arrivals to retailers.
